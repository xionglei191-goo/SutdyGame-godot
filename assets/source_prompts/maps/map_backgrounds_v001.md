## Prompts

### World Overview

Create an original pastel toy-town world overview map for a children's English life-adventure RPG. 16:9, top-down / 2.5D hybrid, bright cozy lighting, clean walking roads, readable landmarks, no logo, no text, no existing IP.

The runtime truth is a large `home + school + town + transport` world, not a poster-like campus screen:

- left/start area: a warm `home` house and front path
- starting focus: `home + Sunshine School + town road`
- major nearby landmark: `Sunshine School`
- inside the school cluster: classroom, library, canteen, art room, music room, playground
- second ring town places: post office, hospital, supermarket, bookshop, restaurant, park, cinema
- outer ring transport: bus station close to the main road, railway station, airport

The first viewport should naturally include both `home` and `Sunshine School`, while the town road clearly connects home, school, shops, and transport. Do not make the school the only oversized center. Leave open paths between home and school so the player can feel a first-trip route.

Do not make it crowded. Strong landmarks may exist, but many secondary A-Z anchor props should stay visually integrated rather than screaming for attention.

### Home Interior

Create an original pastel home interior background for a children's English life-adventure RPG. Cozy morning home scene with a clear pet corner, small table, school bag area, readable open walking space, and obvious low-pressure interaction zones for pet care. Include a visible place where a pet bowl can appear naturally. 16:9, 2D / 2.5D hybrid, no logo, no text, no existing IP.

This is not a classroom or school dorm. It should feel like the child starts the day here before going out into the town.

### School Arrival

Create an original pastel school arrival area for a children's English life-adventure RPG. This is the school-side handoff space after the player reaches Sunshine School from the world overview. Keep it cleaner and smaller than the world overview, with an obvious Mina interaction zone, paths toward classroom/library/playground, and enough open ground for player movement. 16:9, bright, cozy, no text, no existing IP.

This should feel like the school-side welcome scene for `Walk With Mina`, not the whole game world.

Bright cozy classroom with open walking area, two simple desks, a shelf area, a bag-under-desk area, pencil and book learning props visible but not labeled, soft daylight from windows, toy-town proportions, clear uncluttered task zones for drag-and-drop gameplay. 16:9 top-down / 2.5D RPG room background. No logo, no text, no existing IP, no brand elements.
