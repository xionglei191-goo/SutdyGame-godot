## Prompt: Home Pet Corner Refinement

Create an original pastel toy-town home interior background refinement for a children's life-adventure RPG. Focus on the HomeLayer pet corner inside a cozy morning home: a soft pet mat, small low shelf or basket, a clear empty spot where a pet bowl can appear as a separate runtime prop, a clear spot for a pet toy, gentle cleaning/bubble visual affordance area, warm daylight, cozy family-home feeling, and open walking space for a 2D RPG player. Keep the existing home-first adventure mood: the child starts from home, cares for a pet, opens a Welcome Box, and prepares for a first trip into town and school.

The composition should support separate prop sprites for `prop_pet_bowl_v001.png`, `prop_pet_food_v001.png`, `prop_pet_toy_v001.png`, and `prop_soap_v001.png`; do not permanently paint those key item states into the background. Leave clear areas for UI buttons and pet status feedback. 16:9, 2D / 2.5D hybrid, bright pastel colors, clean shapes, no readable text, no logo, no existing IP, no brand elements, no trademark, no watermark.

Negative prompt: existing IP, logo, trademark, brand name, readable text, school worksheet look, classroom dormitory, real toy brand, pet-food brand, store logo, cluttered room, dark mood, scary, mature design, photorealism, watermark.
