## Shared Safety

- Original child-friendly game prop art only.
- No logo, no readable text, no existing IP, no brand elements, no trademark, no watermark.
- Do not imitate any commercial toy, pet-food brand, mascot, packaging, or character design.
- Use transparent background for final PNGs.
- Keep each prop readable at 64x64 and safe for children age 7 to 12.
- Pet-food packaging must be generic and blank: no brand stripe, barcode, nutrition label, school name, or store logo.
