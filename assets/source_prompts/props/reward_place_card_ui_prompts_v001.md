## Shared Safety

- Original child-friendly life-adventure RPG art only.
- No logo, no readable text, no existing IP, no brand elements, no trademark, no watermark.
- Do not imitate any real ticketing brand, transit logo, shop logo, school logo, mascot, or UI from another game.
- Keep final art safe for children age 7 to 12 and readable at small sizes.
- UI/runtime should add any player-facing words separately; generated art should not contain text.

## Prompt: First Trip Ticket Reward

Create a standalone reward icon for `First Trip Ticket` in an original children's life-adventure RPG. Show a charming blank ticket stub with a tiny road curve, small home-to-town travel motif, soft star sparkle, and warm pastel colors. The icon should feel like a child's first adventure from home toward town and school, not like a transit brand. Centered object, transparent background, clean outline, polished mobile-game readability at 64x64 and 128x128, no readable text, no logo, no existing IP, no brand elements, no trademark, no watermark.

Negative prompt: existing IP, logo, trademark, brand name, readable text, real transit logo, barcode, QR code, airline ticket, realistic city pass, school badge, scary, dark, cluttered background, photorealism, watermark.

## Prompt: PlaceCard Ornament

Create a cute clean UI ornament for a `PlaceCard` panel in an original children's life-adventure RPG. The ornament should be a small decorative corner or header flourish combining a soft map pin, curved path, leaf sticker, and tiny sparkle, using pastel coral, mint, cream, and sky-blue colors. It should support shop, town visit, travel stop, and pet-care PlaceCards without looking like a school worksheet. Transparent background, no text, no logo, no existing IP, no brand elements, no trademark, no watermark. Keep it subtle enough that it does not steal attention from title, hint text, or buttons.

Negative prompt: existing IP, logo, trademark, brand name, readable text, real map app pin, store logo, school logo, busy badge, dark UI, scary, cluttered background, photorealism, watermark.

## Review Checklist

- Style pass: matches cozy home, town, pet, shop, trip, and discovery language.
- Age pass: child-friendly, bright, calm, non-scary.
- Copyright pass: no external IP, logos, brands, trademarks, ticketing marks, or readable text.
- Usability pass: transparent PNG, clear silhouette, does not replace runtime text or button semantics.
